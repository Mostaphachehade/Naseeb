Questions hosts ask

Do entrants ever pay anything?

No. Entry is always free, for every giveaway, for every entrant, no exceptions. These plans only apply to hosts publishing a listing.

Why do hosts pay if entrants don't?

You're paying for the audience, the entry infrastructure, and a transparent random draw — the same reason businesses pay for any marketing placement. It keeps Naseeb entirely in the free-promotion model rather than anything resembling paid gambling.

Can I cancel a listing early?

Reach out to us directly and we'll help adjust or close a listing before its deadline if something comes up.

Is there a contract for the Partner plan?

No lock-in — it renews monthly and you can stop anytime.

Naseeb is a free-entry giveaway platform. Hosts pay to list; entrants never pay to enter.
